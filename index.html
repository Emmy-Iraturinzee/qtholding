ABOUT QT HOLDING

Strategic Leadership for Rwanda’s Tech Ecosystem

As a holding company, QT Holding provides strategic leadership, financial oversight, and shared services to our subsidiaries, enabling them to focus on their core missions while scaling their operations effectively.   • Commitment to Rwanda’s Vision 2050

Our Subsidiaries

QT Holding Ltd supports and empowers innovative companies shaping Rwanda’s digital future.

RSwitch

RSwitch is Rwanda’s national e-payment switch, leading the country’s transition to a cashless economy. Through secure, interoperable payment solutions, RSwitch enables seamless transactions for banks, fintechs, and businesses across Rwanda.

QT Global Software

QT Global Software is a software engineering company delivering tailored technology solutions for Rwanda’s unique challenges. From digital transformation to custom applications, we empower organizations to innovate and grow.
